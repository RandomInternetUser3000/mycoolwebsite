Reusable building blocks

Every clip starts from a library of motion templates‚Äîintros, subscribe wipes, text callouts, and caption decks. They snap into a sequence, inherit brand colors, and expose only the controls I tweak per video.

  • Motion presets exported from After Effects via Essential Graphics.
  • Sound design beds layered in and auto-leveled for platform loudness targets.
  • Text styles mapped to Notion content so scripts paste in formatted.

Automation wherever repetition exists

ExtendScript handles tedious steps: caption timing, export queue naming, and thumbnail frame grabs. Scripts expose prompts so I can still make creative calls while shaving minutes off each batch.

  1. Batch import game captures and auto-create sequences per day.
  2. Generate captions from scripts and align to beat markers.
  3. Kick off multi-platform exports with consistent filename slugs.

Toolkit contents

  • 15 motion templates with editable color + text styles.
  • Reusable sound design beds arranged by energy level.
  • Export macros for vertical, square, and landscape outputs.

Planned additions

  • Auto captioning with Whisper + styling in After Effects.
